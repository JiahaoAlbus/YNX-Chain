# Unit economics

Every active-user calculation must separately disclose chain gas, Bundler/Paymaster infrastructure, provider API, compute/data, storage/egress, support/refund load and signing/distribution costs. Free Testnet sponsorship is an explicit capped acquisition expense, never hidden spread or token revenue.

The current first-action policy schema enforces per-operation, per-subject-day and sponsor-day budgets. No live cost samples or revenue history are available, so cost per active user, gross margin and sustainable subsidy are unmeasured. Before scale, operators must record p50/p95 gas cost, failed-operation cost, abuse rate, provider invoice, support minutes and conversion from sponsored first action to retained Testnet use.

Permitted revenue candidates are disclosed subscription, compute/data, provider pass-through, management fee and high-water-mark performance fee with loss carry-forward. Unrealized profit, wash volume, undisclosed spread, repeated high-water reset, secret buyback or mint/burn described as revenue are prohibited.
