# Product KPI and decision framework

Activation is a verified Wallet creation/recovery followed by one successful canonical Product Session or bounded Testnet action. Track D7/D30 retention, authorization and transaction task completion, crash-free sessions, support contacts per active Wallet, replay/abuse rejection rate, provider cost, sponsor cost, gross-margin candidate, public Testnet unique accounts and conversion after first-action sponsorship.

Scale requires sustained SLO compliance, bounded fraud/abuse, positive user comprehension tests for risk and revoke, support load within staffed capacity, and measured unit economics. Kill or pause triggers include unauthorized scope widening, revoke failure, audit-chain corruption, unexplained Paymaster spend, material provider outage, misleading yield display, or inability to provide user exit/export. No dark patterns, fake social proof, fake ranking or hidden risk is permitted.
