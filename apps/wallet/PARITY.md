# Wallet capability parity

Current executable parity is Create, Import/Offline Recovery, multi-account rename/switch/remove, default `ynx1` and internal `0x` compatibility, authoritative Testnet balance/activity, receive QR, signed send, exact authorization review, AI explanation boundary, Connected Apps/Sessions/Devices empty and failure states, audit, biometric gate, background lock, clipboard/screenshot privacy, 12 locales, RTL, light/dark and accessibility semantics.

Protocol-level candidates now cover UserOperation envelopes, Product/merchant/developer/first-action sponsorship budgets, exact Paymaster/EntryPoint/target/selector constraints, Quant/Exchange/DEX mandates, capital product review and selective-disclosure Credentials. They are implemented and tested in `packages/wallet-auth`; they are not yet central-deployed or exposed as a completed on-chain Wallet flow.

Unproven parity remains passkey enrollment on installed devices, deployed Bundler/Paymaster, Guardian recovery, batch execution, stablecoin gas, hardware signer, contract simulation against a deployed node, WalletConnect installed-flow compatibility, live capital positions/exit queues, physical-device accessibility, production signing and stores. Each remains false in release evidence until direct proof exists.
