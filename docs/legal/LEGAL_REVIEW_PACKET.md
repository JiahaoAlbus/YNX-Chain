# YNX Chain Legal Review Packet

Version: 1.0.0-candidate  
Prepared: 2026-07-22  
Status: issue list for qualified counsel; not legal advice or approval

## Materials

Review the draft Terms of Use, Privacy Notice and Acceptable Use Policy; regulatory gap analysis; provider register; YNXT/tokenomics and fee disclosures; staking/liquid-staking/safety-module design; stablecoin/reserve/redemption; bridge/oracle; custody/exchange materials; Trust/appeals; AI governance; marketing claim matrix; support/dispute policy; and launch plan.

## Decisions required

- operating entity/entities, jurisdictions, addresses, age/capacity, governing law, dispute process and official contacts;
- product-by-product role: software publisher, network contributor, custodian, broker/exchange, payment service, issuer, redeemer, data controller/processor, AI provider or other classification;
- YNXT distribution, monetary-value language, transferability, marketing, tax, sanctions and consumer disclosures;
- custody, exchange, DEX/vault, staking, performance-fee and market-integrity obligations;
- stablecoin issuer/reserve/redemption authority and bridge/oracle responsibilities;
- KYC/AML/sanctions, payments/cards, complaints, refunds, suspicious activity and recordkeeping;
- privacy purposes/bases, data map, processors, cross-border transfer, retention, user rights, children and breach notice;
- open-source/project license, contributions, trademarks, media rights and third-party notices;
- warranty, liability, indemnity, suspension, termination, export controls and service-sunset obligations; and
- security reporting safe harbor and regulator/law-enforcement request handling.

## Evidence requested from counsel

Return jurisdiction and product scope, reviewed document versions/digests, redlines, assumptions, unresolved questions, required licenses/registrations, prohibited claims, mandatory disclosures, owner, approval date, expiry/review trigger and a release decision. Do not record privileged advice in public repository files unless counsel approves publication.

## Current boundary

All legal texts remain drafts. No license, registration, issuer relationship, custody approval, reserve opinion, exchange listing, card program, provider approval or regulatory endorsement is inferred.
