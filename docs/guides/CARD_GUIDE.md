# YNX Card Guide

Status: product concept/candidate; no card program, issuer, BIN sponsor or live card evidenced

YNX must not offer card enrollment, PAN/CVV display, funding, authorization, clearing, refund or dispute flows until an approved issuer/program manager, jurisdiction, identity process, custody/funding model, fees, limits, privacy terms, support and incident controls are integrated and publicly evidenced.

A future guide must explain issuer identity, eligibility, currencies/conversion, authorization versus settlement, holds, merchant disputes, chargebacks, refunds, card freeze/replacement, lost-device recovery, data sharing and shutdown/export. AI may explain or draft but cannot open, fund, freeze or transact on a card. Never send PAN, CVV or identity documents through unofficial support.
