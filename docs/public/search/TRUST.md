## Direct answer

YNX Trust is the evidence, request-validity, appeal, correction and transparency
architecture for YNX products. A report, allegation, third-party label, cached
value or AI score cannot silently become authoritative enforcement.

## Evidence and authority

Every consequential record identifies source, authority class, observation/effective
time, version, evidence reference, scope, expiry/review and audit ID. External
references remain bounded inputs unless independently verified by the defined
authority.

## Requests and enforcement

A request affecting access, content or assets needs authority, legal/policy basis,
scope, necessity, evidence, effective time and review. Invalid, overbroad,
expired, wrong-product or unsupported requests fail closed. Stablecoin issuer
controls cannot freeze or seize native YNXT through the current control plane.

## Appeals and correction

Users need notice where lawful, reason, evidence access where permitted, appeal,
status, correction and outcome. Corrections preserve the prior audit record rather
than silently rewriting history. Emergency action remains bounded and reviewed.

## Market integrity

Exchange, DEX and Quant claims must reject fake volume/liquidity, wash trading,
hidden spread, guaranteed return, unverified ranking and unsupported social proof.
Market data and model output disclose source/freshness and cannot assert custody
or settlement.

## Current status

Trust/governance APIs and product candidates exist at varying evidence levels.
Complete central Wallet/Gateway integration, public case center, legal review,
remote deployment and independent public evidence remain component-specific.
